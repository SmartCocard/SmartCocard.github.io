About us

Our company, Smart International Services has been providing various systems related to card payment, such as store sales and e-commerce mainly from Japanese companies in the US since 1995.




Accept from various credit cards.

We know that merchants who accept card-based payments benefit in a variety of ways. Accepting credit and debit cards reduces time spent billing and collecting, and accepting credit cards allows business to compete more effectively in the marketplace.

We offer a wide range of card-based payment options to our customers. Our merchants are able to accept most major credit cards such as: Visa®, MasterCard®, Discover®, American Express®, Diners Club®, JCB®, Diners Club®, Union Pay®
