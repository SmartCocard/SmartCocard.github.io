Merchant Account Application & Solutions

Smart International Services is pleased to be a registered Member Service Provider with several of the industry’s largest payment processors. Having access to multiple platforms gives us the ability to provide numerous solutions to your business that better serve your individual processing needs.

It also allows Smart International Services the ability to present an entirely customized, state-of-the-art processing solution, including the latest, most technologically advanced products and software offerings available today.

Free Credit Card Terminal Rental & Installation Procedure, Free Supply

After your Merchant Account has been approved by our payment processors, you will need a credit card terminal to make card payment transactions.

At Smart International Services, we provide a free rental of credit card terminal along with complementary supply that include thermal paper rolls, bill presenters, tip & check trays, VISA & MASTERCARD Logo Stickers, and more.

Point Of Sale Equipment & Installation Services

Smart International Services offers a full menu of proven processing solutions, payment gateways and point-of-sale systems to meet your business's unique needs.

Point of sale (POS) transaction processing systems are constantly evolving, and Smart International Services keeps pace of offering Revonu, a full service point of sale system for restaurant, retail, and salon available in both tablet and windows based solutions.

E-commerce & Mobile Payment Consultant

Internet Shopping or E-commerce has grown significantly over these past few years making it accessible to many consumers. Merchants have lower initial cost, efficient use of inventory, and since you have access to the consumer’s information marketing will be easier.

Credit card payments is made possible by linking the virtual payment software to your shopping cart, and Apps on your smartphones can allow mobile payments possible.

ACH and Check Guarantee & Settlement Consultant

Automated clearing house (ACH), is the payment method that moves money electronically. In the US, it is used to transfer and pay funds into bank accounts using only bank and account number.

ACH allows you to withdraw automatically the monthly membership fees for your services. The check guarantee system prevents fraud checks from going through with secure validation systems.
